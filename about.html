What is SDG Goal 11?

SDG 11 aims to make cities inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.

Role of Urban Farming

Urban farming helps achieve SDG 11 by providing local food sources, enhancing green spaces, and creating community engagement.

Key Statistics

  • Over 50% of the world's population lives in urban areas.
  • Urban farms can reduce food miles by up to 20%.
  • Community gardens improve social interactions by 30%.

Why Awareness Matters

Raising awareness fosters participation and support for urban farming in sustainable city development.
